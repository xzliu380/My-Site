Volunteering & Charity





Smoke Alarm Walks


I have volunteered since 2019 at Smoke Alarm Walks, helping the community reduce risk of fire injuries and loss before they can happen. I always feel a sense of satisfaction and happiness after each volunteering activity when people express their gratitude, and it is really rewarding to help out different communities. A smoke alarm walk is when a group of three volunteers (one tasked with explaining, one tasked with paperwork, and one tasked with installing a smoke alarm) go out on pre-assigned streets knocking on doors offering to install a smoke alarm for free.
