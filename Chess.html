Story Time!

During one of my first chess tournaments, I had won all three of my previous rounds, and was playing this final round to determing who would be awarded first place. My opponent had used a clever tactic and won my queen which almost certainly meant I would lose this match.

I decided to play on, telling myself that even if I lost, it wouldn't be due to lack of effort.

During the middle-game, my opponent made a terrible blunder and since I played defensively since losing my queen, I actually had a large advantage after this. In the end, I was able to emerge victorious and even though I made a mistake, I learned a lesson that day: never give up.
